About The Company

CALIX Glass and Aluminum Fabrication is a single proprietorship company established in 2017, specializing in glass and aluminum fabrication, project planning, and construction management.

Mission

CALIX Glass and Aluminum Fabrication is committed to delivering high-quality services and materials at competitive market rates while promoting innovative solutions, practical design standards, and client-centered service.

Objectives

CALIX aims to provide industry-competitive construction solutions, continuously improve product development, and cultivate a skilled workforce capable of adapting to modern construction and fabrication standards.

Services Offered

CALIX Glass and Aluminum Fabrication provides specialized construction solutions across residential, commercial, and architectural sectors.

Glass Installation

  • Sliding doors and windows
  • Frameless storefront systems
  • Shower enclosures and partitions
  • Curtain walls and skylights

Aluminum Fabrication

  • Architectural aluminum framing
  • ACP cladding installation
  • Aluminum railings and louvers
  • Custom fabrication solutions

Project Management

  • Construction planning and layout
  • Residential renovation support
  • Site coordination and supervision
  • Supply-to-installation management
